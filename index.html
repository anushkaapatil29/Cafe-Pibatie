Our Story

Where Piba meets Baat

The name Café Pibatie is a poetic fusion of two Hindustani words: Piba (पिबा) meaning "to sip," and Baat (बात) meaning "to talk." Together, they capture the very soul of this space — a place where every sip sparks a story.

Born in the vibrant lanes of Mumbai, Café Pibatie is a haven for the curious, the creative, and the coffee-obsessed. Our walls are adorned with hand-painted murals celebrating Indian art traditions, our menus celebrate the union of Hindustani flavors with global café culture, and our every corner invites you to linger a little longer.

Proudly 100% Vegetarian & Jain-friendly, we believe great food and great conversation are the finest luxuries of everyday life.
